FAQ

Common questions about AI Jurisdiction agents and governance.

What jurisdictions are supported?

Jurisdiction scope is defined per case. Agents operate only on accepted country rules and cross-border compliance.

How do you prevent unsupported outputs?

The judge agent enforces evidence checks and the orchestrator requires citations before finalizing results.

Can we integrate with private knowledge bases?

Yes. Evidence ingestion supports vector indexes and shared repositories for regulatory updates.
