Case Summary: Delivery Contract Dispute

The client entered a delivery agreement requiring shipment of critical parts by May 15. The supplier delivered on May 28.
The contract includes a clause stating that time is of the essence and delays longer than 7 days permit termination.
The supplier notified the client on May 10 about logistical delays and offered a 5 percent discount.
The client asserts that the delay caused a shutdown of operations for 3 days and seeks damages.
